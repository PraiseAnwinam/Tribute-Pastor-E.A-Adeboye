Pastor E.A Adeboye

Tribute to Pastor E.A Adeboye

Biography and Impact

  • 1942 - Born in Ifewara, Osun State, Nigeria, into a humble family with limited means
  • 1956 - Admitted into Ilesha Grammar School, where his academic excellence begins to shine
  • 1967 - Earns a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka (UNN)
  • 1969 - Marries Foluke Adenike Adeboye, a union that will become a foundation of his personal and ministry life
  • 1975 - Joins the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) and becomes actively involved in ministry as an interpreter
  • 1977 - Earns a Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from the University of Lagos; dreams of becoming Nigeria's youngest Vice-Chancellor
  • 1981 - Appointed the General Overseer of RCCG after the passing of the founder, Rev. Josiah Akindayomi
  • 1990s - Begins RCCG's global expansion plan: “one church within five minutes walking distance in every city and town”
  • 2000s - Gains global recognition as one of the most influential spiritual leaders, receiving numerous honorary doctorates and awards
  • 2010 - Leads RCCG's continued expansion into over 190 countries, with thousands of parishes worldwide
  • 2017 - Named among the world's 50 most powerful people by Newsweek
  • 2022 - Celebrates 40 years as General Overseer of RCCG with global tributes from leaders across continents
  • Today - Known globally as “Daddy G.O.,” he remains a humble servant of God, mentor to millions, and a spiritual father to nations
"His ministry reflects a life of humility, holiness, and unwavering faith. Through him, God has raised an army of revivalists and transformed destinies across the earth."
